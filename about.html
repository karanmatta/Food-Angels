Welcome to FoodAngles


At Food Angles, we are committed to making a difference in our community by fighting food insecurity and reducing food waste. We believe that everyone deserves access to nutritious meals, and we work tirelessly to bridge the gap between surplus food and those in need.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to alleviate hunger and provide food assistance to individuals and families facing food insecurity. We aim to create a sustainable food system by rescuing surplus food from various sources and redistributing it to those who need it the most. Through our efforts, we strive to improve the well-being and quality of life for vulnerable populations.

What We Do:

1. Food Rescue: We collaborate with local restaurants, grocery stores, farms, and other food providers to collect excess, fresh, and healthy food that would otherwise go to waste.
2. Food Distribution: Once we have collected the rescued food, we efficiently sort, package, and distribute it to partner organizations such as shelters, community centers, food banks, and schools.
3. Education and Advocacy: We believe in the power of education and advocacy to address the root causes of food insecurity. We raise awareness about food waste, sustainable practices, and the importance of equal access to food.

How You Can Help:

1. Food Donations: If you are a food provider with surplus food, we encourage you to join our network of partners. Your donations can make a significant impact on the lives of individuals and families in our community.
2. Volunteer: We rely on the support of dedicated volunteers who help with food collection, sorting, packaging, and distribution. Y
3. Monetary Contributions: Financial contributions are crucial to sustaining our operations and expanding our reach.
Join us in our fight against food insecurity and help us create a future where no one goes hungry. Together, we can make a lasting impact and build a stronger, more equitable society.

Thank you for visiting our food donation web page and for your support. Together, we can make a difference!
